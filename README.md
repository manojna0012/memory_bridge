# MemoryBridge

MemoryBridge is an **AI-powered cognitive assistant** designed to support elderly individuals living with **early to mid-stage dementia**. The system acts as a *“second memory”*, helping users recognize people, remember important tasks, and maintain independence in their daily lives.

This project focuses on **Inclusive Innovation and Technology for Social Impact**, ensuring that advanced AI technologies remain accessible to elderly users who often face digital exclusion.

---

# Problem

Dementia affects millions of people worldwide and causes progressive loss of memory, cognitive ability, and independence.

Individuals with dementia commonly struggle with:

- Recognizing family members and friends
- Remembering medications
- Managing appointments
- Keeping track of daily routines
- Understanding time or events

These challenges can lead to **social anxiety, emotional distress, and increased dependence on caregivers**.

Many current digital solutions focus mainly on **health monitoring**, but fail to provide **cognitive assistance** tailored for dementia patients.

---

# Solution

MemoryBridge provides an **AI-powered digital cognitive support system** that helps dementia patients recall information and navigate daily life more confidently.

The platform combines **AI recognition, reminders, and conversational assistance** into a single accessible mobile application.

---

# Core Features

## Face Recognition

Users can point their phone camera at a person, and the system identifies them.

The app displays:

- Person’s name
- Relationship to the user

Example:

> "This is Priya, your daughter."

This reduces confusion and helps patients feel more comfortable during social interactions.

---

## Smart Reminders

MemoryBridge provides adaptive reminders for daily tasks, including:

- Medication schedules
- Doctor appointments
- Meals and sleep routines
- Exercise reminders

Notifications continue until acknowledged, ensuring important tasks are not missed.

Caregivers can manage reminders through a dashboard.

---

## Memory Timeline

The app includes a personalized timeline that helps users recall meaningful life moments.

This may include:

- Family photos
- Important events
- Personal milestones

The timeline helps reinforce identity and emotional connection to past memories.

---

## AI Companion

MemoryBridge includes a conversational AI assistant that helps users stay oriented.

Users can ask questions such as:

- “What day is it today?”
- “Who visited yesterday?”
- “When is my next appointment?”

The assistant provides simple, supportive responses tailored for elderly users.

---

## Caregiver Dashboard

Family members or caregivers can manage and support the user through a dedicated interface.

Caregivers can:

- Upload photos of family members for recognition
- Add and update reminders
- Monitor daily routines
- Provide contextual information for the user

This helps caregivers stay informed while reducing their daily workload.

---

# Inclusive Design

MemoryBridge is designed specifically for elderly users who often face barriers when using modern technology.

Accessibility features include:

- Large and simple user interface
- Minimal navigation steps
- Face recognition login (no passwords)
- Text-to-speech support
- Voice interaction for the AI assistant

These design choices ensure that users with **declining vision, memory, or motor skills** can still comfortably interact with the application.
